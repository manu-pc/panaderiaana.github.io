// Galician empanadas

The taste of Galician tradition

Our homemade empanadas are prepared with thin, crispy dough and fillings made with local produce. You can choose from scallops, octopus, cod, meat, tuna or even apple for a sweet touch. Each one reflects the authentic flavour of the Costa da Morte.

// Empanadillas

Tasty, practical and always homemade

Perfect to take away or enjoy on the spot, our empanadillas are prepared with the same homemade dough as our large empanadas. Available in classic flavours such as ham and cheese, meat and tuna. Small in size, big in flavour.

// Pastries and puff pastry

Freshly made every morning

We prepare daily croissants, napolitanas, ensaimadas and a wide variety of puff pastry sweets. All made with quality butter and a touch of love, ideal to accompany our coffees or take home.

// Cakes and sponges

We sweeten your most special moments

We make homemade sponge cakes and custom cakes for birthdays, celebrations or any special occasion. You can choose flavours, fillings and decoration to your liking. Each cake is handcrafted with fresh, natural ingredients.

// Pastries

Little delights for any moment

From cream pastries to millefeuille or fruit tartlets, at Panadería Ana we make a wide variety of sweets with traditional recipes. Perfect to accompany your coffee or enjoy during a relaxed afternoon in Cee.

// Coffee

Your moment to unwind

In addition to our baked goods, we serve freshly brewed coffee to enjoy in our shop. An ideal option to share a pleasant moment.
