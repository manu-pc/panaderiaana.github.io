// Galician Tradition

A story of flavour, from the heart of Galicia

Although cheesecake has ancient roots (the Greeks already offered cheesecakes to Olympic athletes), in Galicia it found its own character. The combination of local cheeses, fresh eggs and artisan care gave it a unique identity.

At Panadería Ana we continue that tradition: we bake each cake as if it were the first, caring for the creamy texture and balanced flavour that so defines us.

// Special flavours

A modern touch

At Panadería Ana we believe that tradition is not at odds with innovation. Every cake is born from a homemade recipe that we care for lovingly: fresh ingredients, patient baking and that creamy texture that defines us. From that authentic base, we go a step further to surprise those looking for something special.

That is why we have created our special cheesecakes: Lotus, with its spiced sweetness; Pistachio, delicate and creamy; Kinder, for chocolate lovers; and Red Berries, fresh and fruity. A modern touch that respects the essence of homemade cheesecake and celebrates our passion for baking.
