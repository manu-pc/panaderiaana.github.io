Our Galician Cake: the perfect mix of tradition and innovation.

// Tradición Gallega

Galician essence in each byte.

Our Galician Cake is our most beloved creation, a fusion of everything that celebrates galician baking tradition: almond cake, with its century-long history, and delicious semicurated galician cheese, with its unique flavour.

Here at Panaderia Ana we wanted to join our two specialties in a new dessert, repressentative of our native land: a rustic base of almonds topped with the sweetness of cream cheese.

//

Two traditions in one cake

The perfect balance between almond cake and Galician cheese.

// Traditional wisdom

A clash of flavours and textures.

Our galician cake starts with a base of traditional almond cake, made from the best-quality almonds crushed to a flour, mixed with fresh eggs and our own special touch which we've mastered through the years. This base hidrates itself with the cream cheese, making for a delicious and complete dessert.

On top of our almond base we serve a layer of cheesecake made from traditional Galician semicurated cheese, which rises with heat during baking, making for a mousse-like irresistible and creamy texture. This makes for an incredible contrast: the sweetness of almond cake mixed with the unique, delicious cheese flavour. The crunchy, grainy almond base with the creamyness of the cheesecake.

The perfect contrast: almond cake base and artisanal galician semicurated cheese.

// Tradición y Modernidad

Un homenaje a nuestras raíces

Mientras que nuestras tartas de queso modernas (Lotus, Pistacho, Kinder y Frutos Rojos) exploran nuevas combinaciones de sabores, la Tarta gallega es nuestro tributo a lo auténticamente gallego.

Esta creación no sigue tendencias internacionales, sino que se arraiga en lo nuestro: la almendra de los bosques gallegos y el queso de nuestras vacas, criadas en los verdes pastos de nuestra tierra. Es la respuesta a quienes buscan el sabor de Galicia en estado puro.

Base de almendra jugosa
Elaborada las mismas almendras exquisitas de nuestras tartas de Santiago, creando una textura húmeda y rica que se hidrata perfectamente con el queso.
Queso del país
Queso semicurado gallego, otorgando un sabor más intenso y una experiencia que ningún amante del queso se puede perder.
